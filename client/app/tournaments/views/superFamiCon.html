Super FamiCon

Recursion’s first out-of-state tournament in Greensboro, North Carolina, Super FamiCon 2016 was a Nintendo fan convention and tournament with over 600 attendees. Recursion was contracted to produce the main stream for the event as well as assist in logistical planning and scheduling for the tournament. In addition to local talent from the North Carolina Smash scene, Super FamiCon featured top player attendance from the likes of DruggedFox, Colbol, and n0ne.


After a dominant performance over Colbol in Winners Finals, DruggedFox moved onto Grand Finals without dropping a single game. From narrowly losing to him in Winners Semis, n0ne proceeded to come back through losers bracket and defeat Colbol in an nail-biting set to qualify for Grand Finals. n0ne carried this momentum through Grand Finals, where his Falcon dominated DruggedFox in a 6-0 performance to take home the title.
